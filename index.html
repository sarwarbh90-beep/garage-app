Car Care Tips 💡
Never ignore a 'Check Engine' light. Changing your engine oil every 5,000–10,000 km prevents expensive engine overhaul costs.
If you feel a 'jerk' while shifting, check your transmission fluid immediately.
In Bahrain's heat, clean your cabin air filter every 6 months.
Squeaking noises when braking usually mean your brake pads are worn out.
Check tire pressure monthly. Properly inflated tires improve fuel efficiency.
High temperatures cause battery fluid to evaporate faster.
The Bahrain heat puts extreme stress on your radiator.

Sensor & Diagnostic Guide ⚠️

🔴 Oxygen Sensor (O2)

Faulty O2 sensor drops your mileage and causes black smoke.

🟡 MAF Sensor (Mass Air Flow)

If your engine vibrates while idling or stalls suddenly, your MAF sensor might be dirty.

🟢 ABS & Speed Sensor

If the ABS light stays on, your wheel speed sensors might be failing.

🔵 Coolant Temp Sensor

This sensor protects your engine from overheating.

🟠 Crankshaft Position Sensor

Engine won't start even if it cranks? This sensor controls engine timing.
